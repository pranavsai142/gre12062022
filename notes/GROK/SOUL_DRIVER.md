## Final status (read this first)

The Internet Party product is **discontinued**. The public site is a shut-down surface. There is no ongoing mission to deliver parallel direct democracy at scale via this repository.

**Why (one line):** Parallel process without parallel power is a simulation; this stack never had power, and power is not crowdsourced from the open internet as a single demos.
